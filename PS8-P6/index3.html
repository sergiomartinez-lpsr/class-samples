Intro

LPS Richmond takes part in the Bay Area Charter Schools Athletics Conference. The Pumas have earned a reputation for teams that continue to do outstanding season after season. Our school has won multiple titles this season including soccer and volleyball. Not only do Pumas exceed in athletics, but they also exceed in academics. In LPS Richmond, the required GPA to take part in a sports team is a 2.4. The majority of the student athletes surpass that GPA.

Scores and more

The Boy's Baseball team ended the season with a 12-4 record and were runner-ups for the championship.

Our Girls Varsity Volleyball took the championship this season after defeating LPS Hayward 3-0. They ended their season with an impressive 16-3 record. They will now play Oakland Tech Saturday for a chance to play state level.
